Kiwi Strawberry Smoothie

Description

This is a recipe for a Kiwi Strawberry Smoothie that takes 5 minutes to prep and serves 2 people. It has 5 different ingredients that you can find at your local grocery store. This recipe is very simple as it has only one step needed to complete it.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 6 strawberries
  • 1 kiwi
  • 1/2 cup vanilla frozen yogurt
  • 3/4 cup pinapple and orange juice blend

Steps

  1. Place the banana, strawberries, kiwi, vanilla frozen yogurt, and pineapple and orange juice blend in a blender. Blend until smooth.
